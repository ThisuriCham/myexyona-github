1. Who am I ?

    I'm EXYÓNÁ, who helps you to find gender of your name and the effect of your name to your life.

2. What is this "Finding Gender of your name" ?

    When you enter your name (following instructions), you can get the gender of your name whether it belongs to female gender or male gender

    Output will be in percentage terms, then your name belongs to the highest percentage gender.

    If your name get equal percentages (e.g.:- 50% for female and 54% for male) that particular name belongs to unisex (i.e. that name can be used by both male and female)

    Specially, this task, "Finding Gender of your name" will give the highest accuracy for Sri Lankan names.

3. What is this "Finding Effect of your name" ?

    This task is actually a astrological task. In astrology, we can find the "Gana" of a name. In that manner, effect of that name to our life can be found.

    In "Gana" there are 8 categories, 4 of them give good results and others give bad results. If a name not belongs to these 8 categories, astrologers say that name can give both good and bad (mix) results and call it as a neutral name.

    So here I have summarized above theory, and given your results under three categories called; good, bad and neutral. (i.e. if your entered name belongs to one of four categories which give good results, I categorize that name under good. If it belongs to one of four categories which give bad results, I categorize it under bad. If it not belongs to any of those eight categories, I categorize it under neutral.

4. Does this task, "Finding Effect of your name" give the highest accuracy only for Sri Lankan names ?

    No, this task performs well for different names from different regions

5. Is this task, "Finding Effect of your name" only for personal names ?

    No, you can enter any name here and check its result, e.g.:- If you start a new business, you can check whether its name is good for you and your business or not.

6. What are the benefits of using this application ?

    If you are a parent, you can check the gender of your newly born child's name and can get an idea whether it is suitable to your baby or not.

    No need to pay money for astrologers, save your pocket and just use this application to find the effect of your name/ your baby's name.

    Highest Accuracy

    Less time consumption and Efficiency

    If you are going to start a new business you can check whether its name is good or not.
